General Questions

Q:What services do you offer?

Ans: We offer a wide range of pharmaceutical services including prescription, refills,medication reminders,and online consultations with pharmacists.

Q:How do i add my medication to the app?

Ans:After loggin in,go to the mediaction page under "My profile".You can search for your medication by name and select the appropriate one from the list.Enter the dosage and schedule.

Ans:To schedule a consultation,go to the "Pharmacist Consultation Section",choose your preferred consultation type,select a convenient time slot,and provide details about your health concerns or questions.Confirm your appointment.

Practitioner Questions

Q: How do i register as a pharmacist on your platform?

Ans: To register as a pharmacist, go to the “Practitioner“ section on the home page. Fill out the required information, including name, contact details, license number, and professional background.Once you submit your registration, our team will review your credentials and notify you upon approval

Q: What type of services can i offer as a pharmacist?

As a registered pharmacy, you can offer various services, including medication consultations, drug interaction checks, health advice, and prescription management. You can provide these through phone calls, video consultations, or in person meetings, depending on your preference and availability.

Q: Are there any fees for using the platform?

Ans: HEALTHGUARD charges a small commission on each consultation to cover operational costs. The exact percentage will be detailed in the terms of services during registration. There are no upfront costs or subscription fees for registering as a pharmacist

Q:Can i set my own consultation fees?

Ans: Yes, you have the flexibility to set your own consultation fees. Go to the “Consultation Fee” section in your account settings and specify the charges for different types of consultation.

Q:How do i receive payments for my own consultations?

Ans: Payments for consultations are processed through our secure payment system. You will need to provide your bank account details or set up a payment method in the “Payment Settings” section of your account. Payments are typically disbursed on a weekly basis.
